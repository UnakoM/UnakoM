THE BEAUTY OF SUNFLOWERS IN HEALING YOU AND BRIGHTENING YOUR DAY.

BY: Unako Mtjekelo.

I think sunflowers are the most beautiful plants in the world. Hi everyone my name is Unako Mtjekelo and my favourite flowers are sunflowers. Sunflowers make me so happy, they brighten my day. I love those beautiful yellow petals and the dark brown center of the Sunflower. When i see a sunflower i feel like im floating and everything starts feeling very virtual and unreal. For me, if i am having troubles in life i would gather around some sunflowers and after a few hours of medidtation with my sunflowers i will be truly happy and find ways to fix my problems calmly. I think that sunflowers truly explain the type of person i am. For example, when they get sunlight and water they shine and stand tall but as soon as they dont get water or they are placed in the shade. Their heads immediatetly face the ground then the sunflowers look very sad. Just like me when i am around things i love i shine and stand tall, but as soon as those things are taken away from me i slouch and get very sad. THAT is the main reason why i love sunflowers because they make me feel like the sunflower and i have something in common. THANK YOU. I LOVE SUNFLOWERS.

If you give me a sunflower my face will glow so much and my smile will become huge.
